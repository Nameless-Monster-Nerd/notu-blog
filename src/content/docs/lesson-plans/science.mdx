# 🔬 Science

Curriculum-aligned science worksheets. Each topic has a lesson page and a printable worksheet.

## Topics

### [Matter & Particles](/lesson-plans/science/matter-particles)
What is matter made of? Atoms, molecules, states of matter — foundational concepts with real-world examples.

### [Energy in Motion](/lesson-plans/science/energy-in-motion)
Kinetic and potential energy, conservation of energy, and how energy moves through systems.

### [Waves, Sound & Light](/lesson-plans/science/waves-sound-light)
Properties of waves, how sound travels, the electromagnetic spectrum — from studio to classroom.

### [Earth Systems](/lesson-plans/science/earth-systems)
The geosphere, hydrosphere, atmosphere, biosphere — how Earth's systems interact.

### [Ecosystems](/lesson-plans/science/ecosystem-web)
Food webs, energy flow, biodiversity, and how living things depend on each other.
