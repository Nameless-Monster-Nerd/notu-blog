# 💻 Algorithms in Everyday Life

**Grade:** 6-8 · **Standard:** ISTE CT.A · **Read time:** 5 min

## 🌟 Big Idea

An **algorithm** is just a step-by-step recipe for solving a problem. You use algorithms every day — when you follow a recipe, give directions, or even tie your shoes!

> 💡 **Did you know?** The word "algorithm" comes from the name of a 9th-century Persian mathematician, **Al-Khwarizmi**. His book on calculation methods was so influential that his name became the word we use today!

## 🧪 Core Concepts

### What Makes an Algorithm?

| Property | What It Means | Example |
|----------|---------------|---------|
| **Input** | What goes in | Raw ingredients, data |
| **Steps** | Clear instructions | Mix, bake, cool |
| **Output** | What comes out | Finished cake, answer |
| **Definiteness** | No ambiguity | "Bake at 350°F" not "bake until done" |
| **Finiteness** | Must end | Has a stopping point |

### Algorithms Around You

| Everyday Algorithm | Steps | Computer Equivalent |
|-------------------|-------|-------------------|
| Making tea | Boil water → add tea → steep → pour | Input → Process → Output |
| Morning routine | Wake → brush → dress → eat → leave | Sequential execution |
| Finding a book | Search by title → locate → checkout | Database query |
| Audio mixing | Balance levels → EQ → compress → export | Signal processing chain |

### 🎵 Lab Connection: Audio Algorithms

The recording studio runs on algorithms:
- **Compression algorithm** — Detects volume above threshold, reduces it by ratio
- **Reverb algorithm** — Simulates sound bouncing off walls
- **EQ algorithm** — Boosts or cuts specific frequency ranges
- **Noise gate algorithm** — Silences audio below a threshold

Every plugin in your DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) is an algorithm!
