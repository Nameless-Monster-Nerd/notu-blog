# 🎵 Music & Sound

From the Lab floor. Production breakdowns, artist stories, gear talk, and the science of sound.

## Welcome to the Lab

Mohikontok Sound Lab opened its doors at 1389 Kearney Ave, Bronx, with a simple mission: give artists a space to create without the corporate price tag. Recording, rehearsal, production — real gear, real vibes, real results.

### What We've Got

- **Recording** — Track, mix, master. From demos to albums.
- **Rehearsal** — Bands, solo artists, dance practice. The space works for you.
- **Production** — Beat-making, songwriting support, arrangement help.

### The Philosophy

> *"Teach a practical skill that makes any content creator better — the studio is where they apply it."*

Every session at the Lab is a learning opportunity. You don't just record — you learn *how* to record. You don't just rehearse — you learn *how* to perform.

### Artists We've Worked With

- Bronx-based hip-hop artists
- Bengali folk musicians
- Dance crews
- Singer-songwriters
- Podcasters
