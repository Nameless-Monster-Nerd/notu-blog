### Welcome to the Briefcase
*July 19, 2026*

Welcome to **notu.us** — Mr. Nas's Briefcase. This is where I keep everything: lesson plans, culture essays, studio skills, dance notes, and the stories that don't fit anywhere else.

The name "notu" comes from the Bangla word **নট** (nôṭ) — a dancer, an actor, a performer. It's also a nod to "note" — as in musical notes, as in taking notes, as in "noted."

This site is a living document. It grows as I teach, as I learn, as I build. Check back often — there's always something new.

— Mr. Nas
