# 🛠️ Studio Skills

**Learn. Build. Share. Grow.**

Welcome to the Studio Skills workshop — a community-powered knowledge base for DIY musicians, home studio owners, bedroom producers, and anyone who wants to make their space sound better.

## 🎯 What This Is

Every guide here comes from **real experience** at Mohikontok Sound Lab — what worked, what didn't, what we'd do differently. No fluff, no affiliate spam, just practical knowledge you can use today.

## 🤝 The Community Vibe

This isn't a one-way lecture. **Every guide has a comment section.** 

- ✅ **Ask questions** — stuck on a build? Drop a comment
- ✅ **Share your wins** — finished a DIY panel? Post a photo
- ✅ **Correct us** — found a better way? Teach us
- ✅ **Collaborate** — find other Bronx DIY builders, swap tips, share gear

> *"The best thing about building a studio is the people you meet along the way."* — Mr. Nas
