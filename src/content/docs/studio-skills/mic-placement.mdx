# 🎙️ Mic Placement for Vocals, Drums, Amps

**Level:** 🟢 Beginner | **Read Time:** 10 min

> *"The best mic in the world sounds terrible in the wrong position. A $100 mic in the right position sounds like a million bucks."* — Mr. Nas

---

## 🎤 Vocals

### The Setup
- **Mic:** Large-diaphragm condenser (if you have one) or dynamic (SM58)
- **Distance:** 6-12 inches from the mouth
- **Height:** Slightly above nose level, pointed slightly down at the mouth
- **Pop filter:** 2-3 inches from the mic, not touching

### The Technique
1. **Back off for loud parts** — when belting, move 8-12" back
2. **Move in for quiet parts** — 4-6" for intimate sections
3. **Off-axis for sibilance** — if "s" sounds are harsh, rotate the mic 15-20° off-center

> 🎥 *[Video: Vocal mic placement demo at Mohikontok Sound Lab]*

### Common Mistakes
❌ Too close = proximity effect (boomy, muddy low end)
❌ Too far = room sound (echo, reverb, noise)
❌ Pop filter too far = useless
❌ Mic pointed at nose = nasal sound

## 🎸 Guitar Amps

### The Sweet Spot
1. Start with the mic **1" from the grille cloth**, aimed at the **edge of the speaker cone** (not the center)
2. Center = brighter, more aggressive
3. Edge = warmer, smoother
4. Move the mic in small increments — 1/4" changes the tone dramatically

### Two-Mic Trick
- **Mic 1:** SM57 at the cone edge (close, 1")
- **Mic 2:** Large condenser at 6-12" (room sound)
- Blend to taste. The room mic adds air and space.

## 🥁 Drums

### Kick Drum
- **Mic:** Beta 52A or D112 (inside) + SM57 (outside)
- **Position:** Inside the hole, aimed at the beater impact point
- **Distance:** 1-2" from the beater head
- **Outside mic:** 6-12" from the resonant head, off-center

### Snare
- **Top mic:** SM57, 1-2" above the rim, aimed at the center
- **Bottom mic:** SM57, 1-2" below, inverted phase
- **Angle:** 30-45° off the drum head surface

### Overheads
- **Spaced pair:** 3-4' above the kit, 3' apart
- **ORTF:** 17cm apart, 110° angle — natural stereo image
- **Height:** High enough to capture the whole kit, low enough to avoid too much room

| Kick drum | D112/Beta52 | 1-2" | Inside, at beater |
| Snare top | SM57 | 1-2" | Above rim, center |
