# 🎚️ The $65/hr Soundproof Space Model

**Level:** 🔵 Advanced | **Read Time:** 15 min

> *"A quiet room shouldn't be a luxury. We built ours for $12,000. You can rent it for $65/hr. That's the math."* — Mr. Nas

---

## The Problem

Bruckner Boulevard is one of the loudest roads in the Bronx. 18-wheelers, garbage trucks, motorcycles — 24/7 noise. Recording here without soundproofing is impossible.

**The solution:** A room-within-a-room. Decoupled walls. Mass-loaded vinyl. Green Glue. The whole science.

**The mission:** Build it once, rent it cheap, keep it accessible.

---

## 🏗️ The Build

### The Shell
- **Existing room:** 12' x 14' basement room
- **New room:** 10' x 12' (room-within-a-room)
- **Ceiling height:** 7'6"

### The Wall Assembly (Outside → Inside)
1. Existing concrete wall
2. 1" air gap
3. 2x4 stud wall (16" OC) — decoupled from existing structure
4. 5/8" drywall (first layer)
5. Green Glue (1 tube per 4x8 sheet)
6. 5/8" drywall (second layer)
7. Mass Loaded Vinyl (1 lb/sq ft)
8. 2x4 stud wall (second frame) — fully decoupled
9. 5/8" drywall + Green Glue
10. 5/8" drywall

**Total wall thickness:** ~12"
**STC Rating:** ~65 (conversation outside = barely audible inside)

> 📸 *[Photo: Wall assembly cross-section at the Lab]*

### The Floor
- Existing concrete slab
- Cork underlayment (1/4")
- OSB subfloor (3/4")
- Floating laminate floor
- Area rug + carpet pad

### The Ceiling
- Resilient channel (RC-1) on 24" centers
- 5/8" drywall + Green Glue
- 5/8" drywall
- 6" of rockwool insulation above

### The Door
- Solid core door (1-3/4" thick)
- Drop seal (automatic)
- Weatherstripping on all 4 sides
- STC rating: ~45 (weakest point — always is)

## 📊 Real Results

| Noise Source | Before (dB) | After (dB) | Reduction |
|-------------|-------------|------------|-----------|
| Bruckner Blvd truck | 72 dB | 38 dB | 34 dB |
| Train horn (distant) | 65 dB | 28 dB | 37 dB |
| Conversation in room | 60 dB | — | — |
| Noise floor (silent) | 45 dB | 22 dB | 23 dB |

> 20 dB reduction = 1/4 as loud. 34 dB = barely audible.
