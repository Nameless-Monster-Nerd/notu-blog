# 📋 About Mr. Nas

**Mohammad Nasirullah** — known to my students as **Mr. Nas**.

I run **Mohikontok Sound Lab** in the Bronx — a recording and rehearsal space at 1389 Kearney Ave. But the Lab is more than a studio. It's a classroom, a dance floor, a community hub.

I teach dance (BaFA — open to all), run a non-profit with two NYC school branches, and believe that art is the most powerful tool for education and healing.

My philosophy: **teach a practical skill that makes any content creator better — the studio is where they apply it.**

### What I Do

- 🎵 **Sound Lab** — Recording, rehearsal, production at Mohikontok Sound Lab
- 💃 **Dance** — BaFA dance classes: gymnastics + healing prejudice + heritage
- 📚 **Education** — STEAM lessons, curriculum-aligned worksheets, UDL framework
- ✍️ **Writing** — Culture essays, poetry (kobita), blog stories
- 🏙️ **Community** — Bronx arts, Bengali heritage, non-profit leadership
