## 🎒 Welcome to My Briefcase

Salaam, students, artists, and curious minds. I'm **Mr. Nas** — Mohammad Nasirullah, founder of **Mohikontok Sound Lab** in the Bronx.

This is my digital briefcase. Everything I carry with me as a teacher, artist, and community builder lives here:

- **Culture Lab** — Essays on Bengali heritage, Bronx arts, and dance as healing
- **Music & Sound** — Studio stories, production tips, artist features from the Lab
- **Dance & Movement** — BaFA dance class lessons, choreography, history
- **Lesson Plans** — Curriculum-aligned worksheets and interactive lessons
- **Studio Skills** — Soundproofing, recording, gear guides
- **Blog** — Daily stories, student spotlights, community news
