# 🏙️ Bronx Arts & Community

The Bronx gave the world hip-hop. But the story doesn't stop there.

## The Borough

1389 Kearney Ave — that's where Mohikontok Sound Lab lives. But the Lab is part of a much bigger ecosystem: Bronx artists, dancers, musicians, educators, and community organizers building something real every day.

## Community Partners

- **Bronx Council on the Arts** — Supporting local artists and cultural organizations
- **Bronx Community Boards** — Where neighbors shape the neighborhood
- **BRIO Awardees** — Bronx Recognizes Its Own — celebrating excellence in the arts

## The Movement

Art in the Bronx isn't just performance — it's survival, it's resistance, it's joy. From the boogie-down blocks to the dance studios, from basement recording sessions to community center showcases — this is where culture gets made.

> *The Bronx is the only borough with "community" in its name. We take that seriously.*
