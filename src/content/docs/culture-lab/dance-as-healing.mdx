# 💃 Dance as Healing

Dance is gymnastics. Dance is heritage. Dance is healing prejudice. Dance is freedom.

## The Lab Philosophy

Lab dance class at Mohikontok Sound Lab is open to all — no experience needed, no judgment allowed. Here's what we believe:

1. **Dance is gymnastics** — Movement builds strength, flexibility, coordination. Your body is an instrument.
2. **Dance heals prejudice** — When you move with someone, you understand them. Rhythm breaks down walls.
3. **Dance is heritage** — Every step carries history. Bengali folk, Bronx hip-hop, African diaspora — we honor the roots.
4. **Dance is freedom** — In a world that tells you to sit still and be quiet, dance is rebellion.

## Why It Matters

In the Bronx, our kids need spaces where they can move, express, and belong. Lab is that space. No grades. No competition. Just movement, music, and community.

> *"When you dance, you're not just moving your body — you're moving your story."*
> — Mr. Nas
