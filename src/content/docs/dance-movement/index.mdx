# 💃 Dance & Movement

Fitness dance class at Mohikontok Sound Lab — open to all. Dance as movement, as gymnastics, as heritage, as freedom.

## What is Fitness?

Fitness is dance as:
- **Gymnastics** — Build strength, flexibility, body control
- **Healing** — Movement as therapy, as release, as joy
- **Heritage** — Bengali folk steps meet Bronx street style
- **Community** — Open to everyone, no experience needed

## Class Philosophy

We don't just teach steps. We teach:
- **Rhythm** — Feel the beat in your bones
- **Expression** — Tell your story through movement
- **Connection** — Dance with others, not just beside them
- **Confidence** — Own the floor

## Who Can Join

**Everyone.** Kids, teens, adults. Beginners welcome. No fancy gear needed — just bring yourself and an open mind.
