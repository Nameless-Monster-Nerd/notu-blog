📋 Follow These Steps:
🔍
Step 1 What Is a Pattern?

A pattern is something that REPEATS. Your heartbeat is a pattern. Breathing is a pattern. Walking is a pattern (left-right-left-right).

🥁
Step 2 Clap the Rhythm

Clap this pattern: CLAP ... tap-tap ... CLAP ... tap-tap. Now stomp it. Now snap it. Can you invent your own 4-beat pattern?

🎨
Step 3 Draw a Pattern

Draw a simple shape (triangle, circle, star). Repeat it across the page in a row. Now change the color every other shape.

✂️
Step 4 Pattern Tile Puzzle

Cut out the pattern tiles below. Arrange them to make a repeating pattern. Glue them down.

✏️
Step 5 Pattern Hunt

Look around the room. Find 3 patterns. Draw each one and write where you found it.

Teacher Guide — UDL Adaptations
Use body percussion. For sensory: provide textured pattern cards. For visual: use alternating color strips. For non-verbal: point to the next item in a pattern.
🧠 Sensory: 🥁 Tap different surfaces in a pattern. Feel the vibrations. 👋 Clap a rhythm together — let the student lead the pattern!
