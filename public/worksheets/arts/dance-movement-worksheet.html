📋 Follow These Steps:
🔍
Step 1 Movement Speaks

Before words, humans used movement. A wave, a jump, a hug — your body already knows how to communicate! Dance is emotion in motion.

💃
Step 2 Emotion Machine

Show these emotions with your body: (1) HAPPY — jump, arms up (2) TIRED — slow, droopy (3) ANGRY — sharp, stomping.

🎭
Step 3 Body Shapes

Make 3 shapes with your body: (1) Wide and strong (2) Small and tight (3) Tall and reaching. Draw each shape below.

✏️
Step 4 Draw Your Dance

Draw a person dancing. Add arrows to show movement direction. Label how the body moves.

Teacher Guide — UDL Adaptations
Use scarves or ribbons to make movements visible. For sensory: different floor textures. For non-verbal: point to emotion cards and show through movement.
🧠 Sensory: 💃 Move to fast music, then slow. Feel the difference. 🧣 Wave a scarf — watch the movement trail.
