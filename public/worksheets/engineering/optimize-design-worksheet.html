🧠 Big Idea: Iteration: build, test, learn, improve, repeat 📊 Visual Guide: Iteration cycle arrows: Build 🡢 Test 🡢 Learn 🡢 Improve 🡢 (back to Build)
📋 Follow These Steps:
🔍
Step 1 Failure = Learning

Edison tried 1,000 materials for the light bulb. Each failure taught him what didn't work. Failure isn't the end — it's DATA.

🏗️
Step 2 Tower Challenge V1

Build the tallest tower you can using ONLY 10 sheets of paper and tape. Test it. How tall is it? Does it hold a book?

📝
Step 3 Iteration Log

Draw 4 boxes: (1) My first design (2) What happened when I tested (3) What I changed (4) My improved design

🏗️
Step 4 Tower Challenge V2

Build VERSION 2. What did you change? Test it again. Did it improve?

✏️
Step 5 What Did You Learn?

Write or draw: What was the biggest lesson from your tower challenge?
