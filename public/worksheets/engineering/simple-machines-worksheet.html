🧠 Big Idea: The 6 simple machines and how they make work easier 📊 Visual Guide: Visual grid of all 6 machines: Lever, Pulley, Wheel & Axle, Inclined Plane, Wedge, Screw
📋 Follow These Steps:
🔍
Step 1 Learn the 6 Machines

Simple machines make work easier! ① Lever (seesaw) ② Pulley (flag pole) ③ Wheel & Axle (bike) ④ Inclined Plane (ramp) ⑤ Wedge (knife) ⑥ Screw (jar lid)

🔍
Step 2 Machine Hunt

Walk around your home or classroom. Find at least one example of each simple machine. Write or draw where you found it.

⚙️
Step 3 Lever Experiment

Balance a ruler on a pencil (the fulcrum). Put 3 coins on ONE end. Push down on the OTHER end. Is it easier to push near the fulcrum or far away?

✂️
Step 4 Cut & Match

Cut out the 6 machine pictures. Match each to its name and its job.

✏️
Step 5 Design a Machine

Invent your own machine using at least 2 simple machines. Draw it and label the simple machine parts.

Use real objects. For sensory: let students operate each machine. For visual: create simple machine cards with pictures + labels + real examples.
