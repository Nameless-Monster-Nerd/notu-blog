🧠 Big Idea: Food Chains, Producers, Consumers & Decomposers 📊 Visual Guide: Arrow diagram: Sun → Plant → Rabbit → Fox (showing energy flow direction)
📋 Follow These Steps:
🔍
Step 1 Learn the Roles

Every living thing has a job: 🌻 Producers make food from sunlight. 🐇 Consumers eat other living things. 🍄 Decomposers break down dead things.

✂️
Step 2 Cut & Build a Food Chain

Cut out the pictures below. Arrange them in order: Start with the SUN. Then add the PRODUCER. Then add the CONSUMERS. Glue them in a chain.

🔗
Step 3 Connect the Food Web

Draw arrows showing who eats who. Arrow points FROM food → TO eater. Sun → Grass → Rabbit → Fox. Sun → Grass → Cricket → Frog → Snake.

✏️
Step 4 What If?

Imagine all the FROGS disappeared. What would happen to the crickets? What would happen to the snakes? Write or draw your answer.

🎨
Step 5 Color Code

Color the PRODUCERS green. Color the HERBIVORES yellow. Color the CARNIVORES red.
