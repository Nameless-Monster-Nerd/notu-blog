📋 Follow These Steps:
🔍
Step 1 Learn the Rules

The internet is like a neighborhood. Be a good neighbor! ✅ Safe: Tell a trusted adult if something feels wrong. ❌ Not safe: Share your address with strangers.

✅
Step 2 Safe or Not Safe?

Read each scenario. Circle 🟢 GREEN if it's safe. Circle 🔴 RED if it's not safe.

Step 3 Rewrite It Kindly

Mean message: 'You're so bad at this game, go away!' Rewrite it as something kind and helpful.
