You Use Algorithms Every Day — You Just Didn't Know It!

Education Matters Writer 📅 June 30, 2026 ⏱️ 3 min 📂 Technology

Brushing your teeth, making a sandwich, tying your shoes — it's all algorithms!

### What Is an Algorithm? (It's Not as Fancy as It Sounds!) An **algorithm** is just a set of step-by-step instructions to complete a task. That's it! **Your morning routine is an algorithm:** 1. Wake up 2. Brush teeth 3. Get dressed 4. Eat breakfast 5. Pack your bag If you skip a step or do them in the wrong order — things get messy! Same with computers.
💡 **Try this:** Write the algorithm for making a peanut butter sandwich. Give it to a friend and have them follow it EXACTLY. If you wrote "put peanut butter on bread" but didn't say which side... you might get a sticky surprise!
🧪 Hands-On Learning Activity
## 🐞 Learning Activity: Find the Bug! This algorithm has a BUG (a mistake). Can you find it? **Algorithm for making a sandwich:** 1. Open the bread bag 2. Take out two slices of bread 3. Put the bread on a plate 4. Close the bread bag 5. **Eat the sandwich** 🤔 Wait... where's the peanut butter? Where's the jelly? That's the bug! Fix the algorithm by adding the missing steps. **Your turn:** Write a 4-step algorithm for tying your shoes. Test it by reading it aloud and pretending to follow it!
