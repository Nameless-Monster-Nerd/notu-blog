📋 Follow These Steps:
🔍
Step 1 Learn About Waves

Waves carry energy. Sound waves travel through air, water, solids — but NOT through empty space. Light waves can travel through empty space (that's how we see the sun!).

🌊
Step 2 Ripple Experiment

Fill a bowl with water. Drop ONE pebble. Watch the circles spread. Now drop TWO pebbles. What happens when the circles meet?

🎵
Step 3 High vs Low

Hum a LOW note. Feel your chest vibrate. Now hum a HIGH note. Feel your head vibrate. Where does low go? Where does high go?

🔗
Step 4 Match It

Step 5 Draw a Wave

Draw a wave with labeled crest (top), trough (bottom), and wavelength (distance between two crests).
