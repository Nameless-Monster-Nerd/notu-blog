📋 Follow These Steps:
🔍
Step 1 What Is a Ratio?

A ratio compares two numbers. Lemonade = 1 cup lemon juice : 3 cups water (ratio 1:3). If you double it = 2:6 — tastes the same!

🥤
Step 2 Juice Challenge

The ratio is 1:4 (concentrate : water). How much water for 1 cup concentrate? ___ cups. How much for 2 cups concentrate? ___ cups.

🍪
Step 3 Cookie Math

A cookie recipe: 2 cups flour for every 1 cup sugar. Half batch = ___ cups flour + ___ cups sugar. Double batch = ___ cups flour + ___ cups sugar.

✂️
Step 4 Ratio Match

Cut out the ratio pairs. Match each ratio to its equivalent.

🎨
Step 5 Color the Ratio
